## Overview

The **Telemedicine** module allows doctors to conduct virtual check-ups with patients who cannot visit the hospital physically. It includes integrated video calling, chat, and digital prescription capabilities.

### Starting a Session

1. **Queue Notification:**
   Incoming teleconsultation requests appear in your **Doctor Console** queue with a <Icon icon="video" iconType="solid" /> icon.

2. **Launch Call:**
   Click the **"Start Video"** button on the patient card. This will open a secure Jitsi Meet room within the ATAMAN interface.

3. **In-Call Actions:**
   - **Share Screen:** Useful for reviewing X-Rays or lab results with the patient.
   - **Toggle Camera/Mic:** Standard controls for audio/video privacy.
   - **End Call:** Terminates the connection and returns you to the Clinical Decision workspace.

<Note>
  **Browser Permission:** You must allow access to your camera and microphone
  when prompted by the browser for the first time.
</Note>

### Connection Requirements

| Component          | Recommendation                          |
| :----------------- | :-------------------------------------- |
| **Internet Speed** | Minimum 5 Mbps upload/download.         |
| **Browser**        | Google Chrome (Latest Version) or Edge. |
| **Hardware**       | Webcam and Headset with microphone.     |
