## Overview

**Digital Charting** is the primary workspace for nurses and specialists to record ongoing care observations without interrupting the physician's main console.

### Features

- **Visual Body Map:** Click on body parts to tag injuries or pain points.
- **Flowsheets:** Record repetitive data like fluid intake/output or hourly vitals.
- **SOAP Note Entry:**
  - **S**ubjective: Patient's stated symptoms.
  - **O**bjective: Measurable data (labs, vitals).
  - **A**ssessment: Analysis of current condition.
  - **P**lan: Next steps in care.
