## Dashboard Metrics

Gain high-level visibility into hospital operations.

- **Patient Census:** Total admissions vs. discharges per day.
- **Queue Velocity:** Average waiting time before consultation.
- **Disease Surveillance:** Heatmap of top diagnoses (e.g., Flu, Dengue) for public health monitoring.

### Exporting Reports

Admin users can generate monthly PDF reports for:

- PhilHealth Claims Processing
- DOH Compliance
- Departmental Performance Reviews
