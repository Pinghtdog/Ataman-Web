## User Profile

To update your personal information:

1. Click your **Avatar** in the top-right corner.
2. Select **"Settings"**.
3. You can update your **Display Name**, **Contact Number**, and **Password**.

## Facility Configuration

_(Admin Access Only)_

Admins can configure global hospital parameters:

- **Operating Hours:** Set the hours when the queue is open for new tickets.
- **Service Types:** Add or remove specialty clinics (e.g., "Dental", "Pedia", "OB-GYN").
- **Staff Management:** Invite new doctors or BHWs via email.

<Warning>
  Changes to **Service Types** will immediately affect the options available in
  the patient kiosk app.
</Warning>
